BOINK is a retro onchain arcade built for fast, fair games and NFT-native rewards.

COIN FLIP
- Provably fair outcomes with onchain settlement.
- Instant wins/losses, transparent odds, and verifiable history.

NFT STAKING
- Stake your ERC-721s to earn BOO rewards.
- Claim any time, view staked counts, and track pending rewards in-app.

ROADMAP (LIVE -> NEXT)
1) Live: Coin Flip, Wallet Connect, NFT Staking, Rewards UI.
2) Next: Multi-game arcade, seasonal leaderboards, and rarity-based perks.
3) Later: Creator drops, marketplace utility, and cross-chain expansion.

MISSION
Make onchain gaming feel as fun and simple as classic arcade games while keeping the mechanics fully transparent.
